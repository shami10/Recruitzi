Our Features

Real-time Data

All information is available in real-time without any delay to increase productivity and accuracy. Employees know where they need to be for their shift, when they start work and who they are reporting to.

Auto-match Talent

Employees are auto-matched against the posted job orders based on their availability, skill set, location and star ratings. Staffers save time and have better visibility and control over distributed workforce.

Increase Attendance

Employees get instant updates about their work schedule and shifts, they are also provided with directions to reach their workplace to reduce late appearance and no-show issues.

Track Time

No need to print, fax and scan time sheets as you can track and approve everything in real-time. Clock-In and Clock-Out are recorded via mobile application.

Validate Performance

Star ratings are assigned to the employees for their performance.

Automatic Alerts

Recruitzi sends automatic alerts whenever an event requires attention to keep everyone informed.
