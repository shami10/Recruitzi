How It Works

STAFFERS

Download Recruitzi App and Register

Download Recruitzi app on your Android or iOS smartphone and create new account OR register on Recruitzi Website.

Sign In

After downloading the app on your mobile phone Sign In with your username and password.

Post Your Job Orders

When you need to hire staff, login through Recruitzi web portal or mobile app and create your job order. Set the skills you require, schedule for work and post your order.

Professional are Auto matched

After successful order posting employees are automatically matched based on their distance from job, star rating, skills and availability. You can select desired employees and invite them for work.

See Who is Coming for Work

You will be able to see all the employees who are coming for work with their pictures, star rating and other information.

Approve Time Sheets

You will be presented with time sheets for the shifts that have been completed. You can adjust and approve them.

Rate the Employees

While approving their hours you have the option to rate the employees based on their skills and performance.

View Invoices

Invoice will be presented to you for payment as per your pay period with shift details. You can make payment accordingly.

EMPLOYEES

Download Recruitzi App and Register

Download Recruitzi app on your Android or iOS smartphone and create new account OR register on Recruitzi Website.

Sign In

After downloading the app on your mobile phone Sign In with your username and password.

Get work Assignments

You will be notified whenever a work assignment is available that matches your skills and schedule.

Apply for Work

You can check the assignment and confirm your availability for work.

Directions to Work

Map and directions are also available for work.

Clock In & Clock Out

When you are at the work location you will be able to Clock In and start work. At the end of your shift just Clock Out to record your hours.

Select your Payment Methods

You can select your preferred payment method, like direct deposit or pay card for your payment.

Get Paid

You will receive your payment within 10 days or you can use "Pay Now" option to get faster payment.
