Privacy Policy

Last Updated : 10-Oct-2020

We at Recruitzi take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy covers what we do with the personal information you choose to share with us and the data you collect while using the Recruitzi application.

User Account Creation: Because Recruitzi is a professional service, we require certain personally identifiable information during the user account setup process to establish your identity. Your name, email address, occupation, employer and age are all required to create a Recruitzi account. The individual details of your information will not be shared with any third party without prior notification to you beyond what is outlined in this Privacy Policy. Tools are available for members to change this information they’ve shared via the Recruitzi application. Personal information is stored securely on secure database servers. Recruitzi may share anonymous Collective information to our customers in the form of metrics, etc.

Location: Each time you launch the Recruitzi app, we collect your location information to best determine what Jobs to display to you. We may share this information on recruitzi.com in collective form so that potential customers may understand how our members use the service. If you apply to a Job we will show the customer the city you are located in when you applied. If you are accepted to do a Job and perform the Job for a Recruitzi customer the customer will see the specific location where you performed it.

Payment: To accept payment for the Jobs you performed you will need to share the email address associated with your PayPal account with us in order to receive payment.

Recruitzi Customers: Our customers work with us to post Jobs – short-term job opportunities. When you undertake a Job, the location information and work-product you collected and/or generated through answering questions and taking photos becomes the property of that customer. In addition to the content created during a Job, we will share your location at the time you performed the Job, your first name, last initial and your pictures at clock in/clock out.

Transference of Services: If Recruitzi or its assets is acquired, member information would be one of the assets that are transferred to acquire by a third party.

Please note that use of Recruitzi is limited to individuals who are 18 or older. If you’re a parent or guardian and become aware that your minor child is using our service, please contact info@recruitzi.com regarding the removal of the account in question.

Recruitzi may change and update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Should you have any questions regarding the content, please feel free to contact us at info@recruitzi.com.

Recruitzi is designed to streamline the time consuming and wearing processes of temporary staffing and recruitment.
